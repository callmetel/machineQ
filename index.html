Introducing machineQSM (machine quotient): A new Comcast business that leverages the latest in sensor, network, and software technologies to gather, transmit, and create actionable intelligence from the physical world.

LOWER POWER,
BIGGER REACH

Our carrier grade low power network, built on LoRa® technology, provides a low cost way to connect power-challenged sensors and devices.

Leveraging a variety of wireless and wireline technologies, machineQSM accommodates deep indoor and outdoor use cases, enabling cost-effective solutions suitable for a wide range of enterprise applications.
